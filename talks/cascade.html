Moving Forward with the Arabic Design Scene

Speaker  Ahmad Saqfalhait.

In the past decades, design in the Arab world has been heavily influenced by the western schools of Design. 
By the begining of 2011, with the outbreak of the revolutions in the Arab world, the Arabic design scene changed and has been focused to reflect the pulse of the streets inspired by the eastern culture.

Saqfalhait will be talking about his vision for the design scene in the arabic speaking societies, his mission as an academic instructor and how working with students was the most effective way to move forward with the Arabic Design scene.
